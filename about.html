About Beachside Montessori Village PTSA

Who We Are

The Beachside Montessori Village PTSA is a volunteer-led organization dedicated to supporting our school community through events, enrichment, and advocacy. We represent every child and give voice to families, teachers, and students alike.

Our Mission

To make every child’s potential a reality by engaging and empowering families and communities to advocate for all children.

What We Do

  • Organize family-friendly events and fundraisers
  • Support classroom and teacher needs
  • Promote student enrichment and engagement
  • Build community among students, families, and staff
