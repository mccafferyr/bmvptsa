Why Join the PTSA?

Your membership helps fund vital programs, events, and support for our students and teachers. Everyone is welcome – parents, teachers, students, grandparents, and community members.

Membership Benefits

  • Access to PTSA resources and events
  • Voting rights at general meetings
  • Involvement in school decisions and planning
  • Opportunities to connect with other families and staff

FAQs About Membership

  • Do I have to volunteer if I join? Nope! Your membership alone supports our mission.
  • Can I join even if I’m not a parent? Yes! We welcome all caregivers, staff, and community members.
  • How long does membership last? One school year (Aug–July).

Spirit Shirt Info
